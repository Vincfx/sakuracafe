About Us

The blossoming of Sakura Café

At Sakura Café, we blend the warmth of Japanese cherry blossom aesthetics with delicious comfort food and specialty beverages to create a space where memories are brewed and bonds are celebrated. Nestled along the serene ECR highway near Serenity Beach, our air-conditioned, 25-seater sanctuary invites families, travelers, and friends to gather in joy and togetherness.

Our highlights include authentic Sakura-inspired décor with cherry blossom trees and delicate leaf motifs, freshly brewed classic and specialty coffee & tea, gourmet burgers, wraps, sandwiches, and fried specialties, refreshing milkshakes, mocktails, and cool desserts, all with a convenient self-service model for swift and seamless dining.
