Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Sakura Café

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we highly recommend making reservations, especially for dinner and weekends. You can make a reservation through our website, by phone, or by email. We accept reservations up to 30 days in advance.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options on our menu. Please inform your server about any allergies or dietary restrictions when ordering, and our chefs will be happy to accommodate your needs whenever possible.

Is there parking available?

We have limited parking available behind our restaurant. There is also a public parking garage located one block away. We recommend using public transportation when possible, as we are conveniently located near the Sakura Station.

Do you host private events?

Yes, we offer private dining options for special events and celebrations. Our private dining room can accommodate up to 20 guests, and we can customize menus for your event. For larger groups, we also offer the option to reserve the entire restaurant. Please contact us at events@sakuracafe.com for more information.

What is the best time to visit to see cherry blossoms?

While our restaurant is themed around cherry blossoms year-round, the actual cherry blossom season in Tokyo typically runs from late March to early April. During this time, we offer special seasonal menu items and our outdoor garden is particularly beautiful. However, we bring the sakura experience to you regardless of the season!
